Puran Poli
Puran poli is an Indian sweet flatbread that is popular in South India and the state of Maharashtra. It is also known as puran puri, holige, obbattu, bobbatlu, poley, bakshamulu, and boli.
Dosa
A dosa or thosai is a thin, savoury crepe in South Indian cuisine made from a fermented batter of ground black gram and rice. Dosas are served hot, often with chutney and sambar.
Momos
Momos are savory steamed or fried dumplings from Tibet and Nepal, traditionally made with a simple wheat dough and filled with minced meat or vegetables. A highly popular street food.
Paratha
Paratha is a popular, flaky, layered flatbread from the Indian subcontinent, made with whole wheat flour (atta), water, salt, and cooked with ghee or oil.
